Interesting Facts About Bananas

  • Bananas are technically berries, not fruits!
  • There are more than 1,000 varieties of bananas in the world.
  • Red bananas and plantains are just two of the many lesser-known types of bananas.
  • Bananas are the fourth-most important crop in the world, after wheat, rice, and corn.
  • Bananas are the most popular fruit in the United States, with Americans consuming an average of 26 pounds of bananas per person each year.
  • Bananas are naturally radioactive due to their high levels of potassium-40, but the amount is so small that it poses no health risk.
  • Bananas have been featured in popular culture for decades, from the classic slapstick gag of slipping on a banana peel to the famous "Yes, we have no bananas" song from the 1920s.
